### Issue 2: Overly permissive validation — fixed

**Problem**: Validation accepted any string with "BW" and 5+ characters (e.g., "TestBW", "XBWabc"). **Solution**: Stricter validation:
- Weekly: Must start with "C", contain "W", and be exactly 7 characters (e.g., `26C01W02`)
- Bi-weekly: Must be exactly 6 characters with "BW" at positions 2-3 (e.g., `26BW01`)

**File**: `Export_Formatting.m`

**New Logic**:
```m
isWeekly = Text.StartsWith(cycleName, "C") and Text.Contains(cycleName, "W") and Text.Length(cycleName) = 7
isBiWeekly = Text.Length(cycleName) = 6 and Text.Middle(cycleName, 2, 2) = "BW"
```
